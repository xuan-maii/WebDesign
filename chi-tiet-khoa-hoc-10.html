Description

What is the aim of this course?

This course provides a clear, practical framework for formulating and executing business strategy at the level of top consulting firms like McKinsey and BCG. It is ideal for aspiring management consultants, business analysts, and business leaders. You will learn how to choose the best strategic options, calculate their impact, and implement them effectively.

Drawing from over 15 years of experience in consulting and strategic leadership, the instructor shares lessons from 90+ strategic projects that generated $2 billion in additional EBITDA. The course emphasizes practical application through Excel-based case studies and provides access to downloadable resources like spreadsheets and slides.

What will you gain?

  • Learn how to make strategic decisions and calculate their impact
  • Understand implementation methods used by real businesses
  • Gain practical skills in Excel to support your strategic planning
  • Receive a library of ready-made analyses and business cases

Course structure:

  • Improve existing operations
  • Expand your business
  • Enter niche markets
  • Transform underperforming areas
  • Disrupt your own business model

Who should join?

  • Consultants, analysts, business owners, and startup founders

Enjoy the course and feel free to ask questions anytime!
