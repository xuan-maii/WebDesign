What you'll learn

  • Define the business case for change

  • Assess the organizational culture & its impact on change

Description

If you're a project manager, change manager, leader, or individual contributor, this course is designed to help you manage the human side of change. You’ll learn to transition people from the current to the future state while achieving business goals.

Why managing change matters: Organizations face constant change. Managing it well improves alignment, reduces risks and costs, and boosts team performance. This course gives you a road map to guide stakeholders through change.

What you'll learn:

  • Develop a change management plan focused on people

  • Align leaders on the vision and their role as change sponsors

  • Boost employee engagement and adoption of change

  • Evaluate your culture to predict change barriers or supports

  • Prepare the organization through communication and training

You’ll receive practical tools and templates for every step, plus a clear change management framework and a robust toolkit for future projects.

With 20 years of experience leading change at Fortune 500 companies, I’ve partnered with top thought leaders across the U.S. I’m excited to share what I’ve learned with you.

Let’s get started!

CPE Learning Objectives:

  1. Identify roles and best practices in change

  2. Define change management and resistance

  3. Describe strategies to manage resistance and communication

  4. Assess resources needed to drive change
